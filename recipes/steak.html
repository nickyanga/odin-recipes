Salisbury Steak

What is Salisbury Steak?

Salisbury steak is a processed meat dish originating in the United States and made from a blend of ground beef and other ingredients, such as chicken and pork, being considered a version of Hamburg steak.

How to make Salisbury Steak

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make Salisbury steak at home:

  1. Make the Patties
  2. Cook the Patties
  3. Make the Gravy
  4. Simmer the Patties
  5. Serve

Ingredients for the patty.

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/3 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for frying)

Ingredients for the gravy

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme (optional)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced (optional)

Cooking steps

    Step 1: Make Patties

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into 3-4 oval patties, about ½ inch thick.

    3. Step 2: Cook the Patties

  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
  4. Sear the patties for about 3-4 minutes per side until browned. (They don’t have to be fully cooked yet.)
  5. Remove the patties and set them aside.

    6. Step 3: Make the Gravy

  6. In the same skillet, melt 2 tbsp butter. Add chopped onions (and mushrooms if using) and sauté until soft.
  7. Sprinkle in the flour and stir for 1 minute to cook off the raw taste.
  8. Gradually add beef broth while stirring to prevent lumps.
  9. Add Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, salt, pepper, and thyme. Stir well and bring to a simmer.

    10. Step 4: Simmer the Patties

  10. Return the patties to the skillet, spoon some gravy over them, and simmer for about 10 minutes until the patties are fully cooked and the gravy thickens.

    11. Step 5: Serve

  11. Serve hot with mashed potatoes, rice, or egg noodles. Spoon extra gravy over the top and enjoy!
