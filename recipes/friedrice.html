Fried Rice

What is fried rice?

Fried rice is a traditional chinese food that is easily prepared with just a few ingredients that can be easily found at home.

How to make fried rice?

The process is simple â€” preheat your pan before swirling in oil and add in rice , vegetables and a few more ingredients that you like then vigorously tossing them til you achieve the golden-brown color.

Ingredients

Below are the ingredients that is required to make that perfect golden brown plate of fried rice.

  • 2/3 cup of green onions.
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 cloves of garlic , minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups of leftover rice
  • 1 tablespoon of soysauce/oyster sauce depends on preference
  • 2 tablespoon of sesame oil, or to taste

Cooking steps

  1. Step 1

    Assemble ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in green onions and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with ingredients.

  3. Step 3

    Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce or oyster sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.

  4. Step 4

    Serve it hot on a plate and you are done!
